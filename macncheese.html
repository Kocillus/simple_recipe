BAKED MAC'N'CHEESE

DESCRIPTION

It’s nearly the universal comfort food, and there are SO many variations! In fact, I almost didn’t post this recipe, as there are SOOOO many mac and cheese recipes available, and most people already have their favorite way of making it.

Of course, there’s no “right” way or “wrong” way, so I’m here sharing the way I like to make my mac and cheese (the way my family loves it too).

Ingredients

  • 500g of elbow pasta
  • 100g of unsalted butter
  • 100g of all purpose flour
  • 300ml of milk
  • 500g of grated medium sharp cheddar cheese divided
  • 150g of grated Gruyere cheese divided
  • 1/2 tbsp. of salt
  • 1/2 tsp. of black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp. of paprika

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta
  2. Drain the pasta
  3. Make the cheese sauce by combining a fat (butter), and starch (flour), then whisking in the milk products.
  4. Cook the sauce until it’s nice and thick.
  5. Add in shredded cheeses, stir well.
  6. Combine cheese sauce with cooked pasta.
  7. Transfer half of the mac and cheese to a baking dish, sprinkle with more shredded cheese.
  8. Top with the rest of the mac and cheese, top with more shredded cheese.
  9. Bake for 15 minutes, until cheesy is bubbly and lightly golden brown
  10. Ready!
